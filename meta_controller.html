Meta Controller : Intersection 152 : Mass Av.

Instructions

Click on solid-colored nodes to expand immediate children or collapse all children. Right click to see other actions.
To add a new expert to the tree (After creating one in the "Build An Expert" interface): drag the square in the legend and drop above the node you want to add the expert to, as a leaf. Do not add below another expert.
To convert a leaf into a new expert: Rename your node to what you want that expert to be named. Then right-click and select Make/Show Expert.
To move an existing expert: Remove node from the tree, and follow instructions for "adding a new expert to the tree".
